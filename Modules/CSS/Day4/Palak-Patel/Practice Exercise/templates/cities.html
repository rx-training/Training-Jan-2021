About Gujarat

Gujarat is a state on the western coast of India with a coastline of 1,600 km – most of which lies on the

Kathiawar peninsula – and a population of 60.4 million. It is the fifth-largest Indian state by area and the ninth-largest state by population.

Gujarat is bordered by Rajasthan to the northeast, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu to the south,

Maharashtra to the southeast, Madhya Pradesh to the east, and the Arabian Sea and the Pakistani province of Sindh to the west.

Its capital city is Gandhinagar, while its largest city is Ahmedabad.
