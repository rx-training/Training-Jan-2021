CABIN SANITIZATION

Best-in-class cleaning and sanitizing procedures with Boeing approved disinfectants before every flight.

SYNTHETIC SEATS

We are equipping our aircraft with synthetic Leather seats. These non-porous seats make it difficult for viruses and particles to penetrate inside them and can be easily wiped off compared to standard fabric seats.

AIR CIRCULATION SYSTEMS

The cabin air does not circulate from front to back, it only flows from the ceiling to the floor and gets absorbed for filtration.

HEPA FILTERS

All our aircraft use a High Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filter that removes up to 99.9% of airborne particles.

PROTECTIVE MEASURES

Our crew will be wearing protective gear and customers will be provided with a safety kit.

STEPS FOR WEB CHECK-IN

Web check-in on our website www.spicejet.com or using the SpiceJet App.

One check-in bag of up to 15 kg and one hand bag of up to 7 kg are allowed. Add your check-in baggage during the web check-in process.

Boarding pass along with a baggage label will be emailed to you post web check-in. Please ensure the baggage label is properly displayed on your checked-in baggage.

You can also travel with your mobile boarding pass.

Web check-in is mandatory for domestic flights and closes 60 minutes prior to departure. For assistance with check-in or reissuance of boarding pass at the airport or through Reservations, a facilitation fee of INR 200 per passenger will be charged. Bookings made for senior citizens, armed forces, unaccompanied minors and passengers with medical conditions or on a stretcher are exempted from this fee.

SPICE SHIELD FOR A SAFER TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

Ensure that you have multiple digital payment apps on your phone so that you can avoid cash transactions at the airport.

Before travelling, check state-wise health regulations.

ENTRY TO AIRPORT
INSIDE THE AIRPORT

Use of trolleys should be avoided.

Please avoid touching surfaces as much as possible.

Try to use airport restrooms instead of the aircraft ones since aircraft lavatories are compact.

Maintain social distancing within the airport premises and keep sanitizing your hands. Do not use chairs marked ‘Not for Use’.

Please pay attention to airport advisories and communications and adhere to them strictly.

BAGGAGE DROP

  • Baggage drop closes 60 mins prior to departure.
  • Place your bag on the conveyor belt with a properly filled baggage tag stuck to it.
  • Instead of a paper receipt, you will now receive an SMS for your checked in bag.
BOARDING GATE CLOSES 20 MIN PRIOR TO DEPARTURE

Please collect your safety kit kept near the boarding gate. For added protection, middle seat passenger will additionally be provided with a coverall gown. Safety kit must be put on before proceeding for boarding.

You are encouraged to selfscan your boarding passes and display your identity card to the airlines official.

To avoid physical contact, we will reduce the number of people in our coaches by 50%.

Please follow boarding instructions as we are boarding fewer customers at a time to comply with social distancing for safety.

IN-FLIGHT

Meals and sandwiches along with beverages are available for pre-booking on SpiceJet flights, at discounted rates Our on-board menu offers a variety of ready-to-eat products, munchies and beverages to choose from. Payments will be accepted through debit/credit cards only. As per the orders from the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Food and Beverages are only available on flights with duration of 2 hours and above.

Our in-flight magazine and any other reading material will also not be available during this period.

Please limit your movement in the aircraft cabin. Unless absolutely necessary, avoid getting up and visiting the lavatories once you are seated.

DISEMBARKATION

Passengers will now exit the plane from front-to-back. While disembarking, passengers should strictly adhere to social distancing norms.

BAGGAGE COLLECTION

Please keep your face masks on, follow social distancing norms and maintain all precautions while collecting your baggage and exiting the airport. Dispose used masks and gloves in the biohazard waste bins only. These can be found at the terminal exit. Upon arrival, passengers should comply with the health protocols of the respective state/union territory.

DON’T FLY IF YOU ARE UNWELL

The health and safety of our customers and crew members is our top priority. In case you do not feel well or are concerned that you may have Coronavirus, please do not fly until cleared to travel by a doctor. We, at SpiceJet, are completely ready to fly you and are putting all measures together to deliver industry leading cleanliness, prioritise your well-being and innovate for a healthier tomorrow.

DGCA mandates all passengers to wear masks and to maintain social distancing norms at all times during their travel. All passengers must ensure strict compliance of Covid-19 protocols. DGCA Order for Covid-19 protocols can be checked here .
