Market cap of cryptocurrencies surpasses $1 trillion, bitcoin jumps 9% to trade above $37k

Cryptocurrencies are rising and how. The total value of the cryptocurrencies passed the $1-trillion mark for the first time on January 7. Bitcoin, which represents about 69 percent of the total market, rose over 9 percent, surpassing the $37,000-mark for the first time. Interest in cryptocurrencies has soared recently, with investors viewing them as a hedge against inflation and an alternative to the depreciating dollar.
